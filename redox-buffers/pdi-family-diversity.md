Core PDI Family Members (Humans):
- PDIA1 (canonical PDI): 4 Trx domains (a-b-b′-a′), CGHC sites, generalist oxidase/isomerase/chaperone
- PDIA3 (ERp57): 4 Trx domains, CGHC, glycoprotein-specific (calnexin/calreticulin cycle)
- PDIA4 (ERp72): 5 Trx domains (a-b-b′-a′-c), CGHC, large/hydrophobic substrates, high chaperone
- PDIA6 (P5): 2 Trx domains, CGHC, ER stress sensor, Ero1/PrxIV interaction
- PDIA2 (PDIp): Pancreas-specific, insulin folding
- ERp44: 3 Trx domains, CTHC (non-catalytic N-term), pH-regulated retention
- ERp29: 2 Trx domains, non-catalytic, pure chaperone

Catalytic Site Variations:
- CGHC (balanced redox) → most PDI members
