----------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|-------------------------

Self-healing via vascular fibroblasts          | Inferred repair (fossil scars)                 | Both living tissue; α faster turnover

Biomimetic Mercy Mapping:
- α-Keratin → elastic fibers, impact absorbers, self-healing gels (wearables, padding)
- β-Keratin → rigid composites, high-modulus nanofibers (aerospace, panels)
- Hybrid → tunable stiffness + elasticity (adaptive actuators, multi-role composites)
